## Who I am

**Fan-Powered Royalties**. Now I build, teach, and coach at the intersection of
product management and AI capability.

  Precision → Graduation → Production → Presentation)
- **Coach**: 1:1 with product leaders on AI strategy, team building, modern PM workflows

I built **PM-OS** (36 AI Skills for product managers) and **Rhythm** (34-skill
system for engineering squads). Both encode methodology as behavioural contracts.

## Core beliefs that shape what I find interesting

- AI-native PM inverts the loop: **build → test → extract specs**, not the other way
- The best product leaders of the next decade will think in systems and build with AI
- Skills > prompts. Behavioural contracts > one-shot instructions
- Prototypes accumulate knowledge; the Living PRD captures it before it's lost
- Engineering is being compressed, not replaced

## Topics I want to hear about (high signal)

- New capabilities or workflows in **Cursor, Lovable, Claude Code, v0, Replit Agent, Bolt, Windsurf**
- **Anthropic & OpenAI** model releases — but only the ones that change *what builders can do*, not benchmark theatre
- **Prompting techniques, prompt chains, agent design patterns, MCP** (Model Context Protocol)
- **Vibe coding** — case studies, tutorials, frameworks, criticism
- **PM craft in the AI era** — how PMs are using AI to do discovery, specs, prototyping, comms
- **Real teardowns of AI products** — what shipped, why it works (or doesn't)
- **Founder/builder essays** with strong opinion and lived experience (Lenny, Ethan Mollick, Latent Space, Every, Stratechery)
- **Squad operations & modern SDLC** — sprint cadence, retros, rituals adapted to AI
- **Coaching and product leadership** — career frameworks, hiring, team design

## Topics that score lower (deprioritize)

- Pure benchmark / leaderboard updates without product implications
- Crypto / web3 unless directly tied to AI tooling
- Generic "10 ChatGPT prompts" listicles
- Funding round news without product substance
- Enterprise sales pieces that don't help a builder
- Academic papers without a clear "so what for builders"

## Format I want

- 6 items max, each one is a short headline + 1–2 sentence "why this matters for me"
- Prefer **one strong item** over three weak ones — quality over completeness
- Diversity across sources (don't give me 6 Reddit threads)
- If nothing is good today, say so honestly. Don't pad.
